Mexican Lasagna

Description

It was a rainy cinco de mayo afternoon, and I was in the mood for something zesty.

So I said, "I think I should go for some italian, but it's cinco de maio, so I better do some mex mixing."

Ingredients

  • 14 corn tortillas (duh!)
  • 2 cups of shredded Mexican cheese (I prefer cheddar and Menonita cheeses, but whatever you can get)
  • 2 tbsp of fajita seasoning
  • 1 can of tomatoes
  • 1 can of black beans (gas warning)
  • 2 cups of salsa (go crazy with the spiciness here)
  • 2 cups shredded chicken
  • 1 small onion, chopped

Preparation

  1. grab a crockpot, spray with plenty cooking spray
  2. lay down a layer of tortillas
  3. spread a layer of tomatoes
  4. sprinkle onions
  5. add a layer of beans
  6. add pleny of salsa
  7. apply seasoning here, go crazy (or should I say "loco")
  8. add chicken
  9. cover everything with a layer of tortillas
  10. add a layer of cheese
  11. rinse and repeat above steps until top of the pot is reached, at the top add plenty of salsa and cheese blend
  12. cook on low for 3 hours, then crank up to high for the final 45 min

Top with some sour cream and avocado, maybe some guacamole if you dare.

Yield: 6 people

Total time: 4 hours
